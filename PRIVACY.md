# Privacy Policy — IsItGood?

**Last updated: June 2026**

IsItGood? ("the app") is a game-discovery app that provides AI-generated review
summaries, price deals, and an AI chat about games. This policy explains what data
the app collects, how it is used, and your choices.

Contact: **a01068079952@gmail.com**

## Information we collect

When you create an account and use the app, we store:

- **Account information** — your email address and username. Your password is never
  stored in plain text; it is stored as a one-way **bcrypt hash**.
- **Saved games** — the games you choose to save to your account.
- **Preferences** — your in-app settings (e.g. summary length, tone, font size,
  deal-display and sale-alert options).
- **Chat and summary requests** — the game you are viewing and the messages you send
  in the AI chat, which are processed to generate a response.

We do **not** use advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs, and we do **not** sell or
share your personal data for marketing.

## How we use your information

Your information is used solely to provide and personalize the app: to authenticate
you, store your saved games and preferences, and generate AI summaries and chat
responses tailored to your settings.

## Third-party services

To provide its features, the app sends limited data to the following services:

- **Google Gemini (Generative Language API)** — receives game titles, review snippets,
  and your chat messages to generate summaries and chat responses.
- **RAWG** — game metadata (no personal data sent).
- **IsThereAnyDeal** — game pricing (game titles sent; no personal data).
- **Tavily** — web search used to gather review context (game titles/queries sent).

The backend and its PostgreSQL database are hosted on **Railway**. Data is transmitted
over **HTTPS**.

## Data retention and deletion

We retain your account data while your account is active. You can **permanently delete
your account at any time** from within the app (Settings → Danger Zone → Delete
account). Deleting your account removes your account information, saved games,
preferences, and any cached summaries. You may also request deletion by emailing the
address above.

## Security

Passwords are hashed with bcrypt, sessions use signed JSON Web Tokens, and all network
traffic uses HTTPS.

## Children

The app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal
information from them.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by an
updated "Last updated" date above.
