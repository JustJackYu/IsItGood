# Deleting your IsItGood? account and data

**App:** IsItGood?  **Developer:** Juhyeon Yu

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time, directly
in the app.

## How to delete your account

1. Open the **IsItGood?** app and sign in.
2. Tap **Settings** in the bottom navigation bar.
3. Scroll to the **Danger Zone** section.
4. Tap **Delete account**.
5. Type your **username** to confirm, then tap **Delete**.

Your account is deleted immediately upon confirmation.

If you cannot access the app, you may instead request deletion by emailing
**a01068079952@gmail.com** from your account's email address.

## What is deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes all of your data, including:

- Your account information (email, username, hashed password)
- Your saved games
- Your preferences and settings
- Any cached AI summaries associated with your account

## Data retention

This data is deleted immediately when you confirm deletion. No account data is
retained afterward.
